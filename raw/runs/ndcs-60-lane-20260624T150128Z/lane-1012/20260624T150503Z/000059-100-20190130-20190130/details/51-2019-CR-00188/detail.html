2. Dangerous Weapons - Ward, A dangerous weapon or firearm was seized. The dangerous weapon or firearm, to-wit: Smith & Wesson Special Revolver, serial number DCF0059637, is forfeited to the jurisdiction in which the arrest was made or the charge arose. The dangerous weapon or firearm shall be placed in the custody of the arresting law enforcement agency. Pursuant to N.D.C.C. 62.1-01-02, the law enforcement agency with custody shall use its best efforts to determine if the firearm was obtained from any innocent owner, and if so, return the gun to the innocent owner pursuant to the procedures outlined in N.D.C.C. 62.1-01-02(3). If no innocent owner is discovered, or the innocent owner fails to respond as required in N.D.C.C. 62.1-01-02(3), the agency shall dispose of the firearm by sale at public auction. If no bids from eligible persons are received within six months from when bidding opened on the firearm, or if the agency director, sheriff, chief of police, or a designee from the office certifies the firearm is unsafe for use, the dangerous weapon or firearm shall be destroyed by the arresting law enforcement agency pursuant to the procedures outlined in N.D.C.C. 62.1-01-02. 11/15/2019, Active 11/15/2019
